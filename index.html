Escape The Mirror

This game is an experimental project I created while studying game development. Originally, the task was to recreate a classic brick-breaking game, but I attempted to incorporate it with my original field of study, resulting in a more stylized background. In this game, the gravity in the upper half of the game interface is completely reversed. Players need to control the paddles on both sides to strike the ball and eliminate all the bricks to escape the mirrored space. The outcome of the game, whether success or failure, will lead to certain differences in the settlement interface.
